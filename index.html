Lu Zhang 张璐
lu (dot) zhang [at] ia (dot) ac (dot) cn

I am an Associate Professor at Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences. My research interests include computer vision, machine learning, and robotics.

I have a great passion for robotics, especially in how robots interact with & learn from the real world. Currently, I am dedicated to exploring innovative solutions that enhance robotic capabilities in open environments.

Recently, I have been practicing playing soccer in my free time, as it helps me stay energetic and healthy. I also love Souls-like games, they inspire me to constantly overcome new challenges.
